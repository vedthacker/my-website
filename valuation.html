VALUATION FRAMEWORK

Depending on asset class and purpose, our reports apply one or a combination of the Market Approach, Income Approach and Cost Approach. Each report includes scope, assumptions, data references, site observations and reconciliation logic so stakeholders can review the valuation with confidence.

Market Approach

Comparable evidence, market adjustments and transaction benchmarking for credible market-linked conclusions.

Income Approach

Income capitalization or discounted cash flow methods for income-generating properties and business-linked assets.

Cost Approach

Replacement/reproduction cost less depreciation for specialized assets and engineering-intensive plant and machinery.
